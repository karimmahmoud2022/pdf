HAIR TRANSPLANTATION WITH FUE METHOD INFORMED CONSENT FORM

I was informed about my disease in the clinic I applied for hair transplant treatment. I was informed
about the condition of my hair and all the side effects that may arise from the planned treatment,
medical tests and medications taken.


Detailed information was given about the medical, surgical and operative treatments used and possible
side effects (complications: itching, acne, pain, skin necrosis, etc.) and it was fully understood.


I got all the information about analysis methods, medical and surgical use treatments. During the
procedure, I was informed about local anesthetics, including information about narcotics and
anesthetics.


I am aware that spontaneous events can lead to negative consequences if smoking and alcohol are not
avoided for 14 days and sports are not avoided for 4 weeks after the surgery.


The specialist gave me all the information I needed about possible post-operative complications that
could arise. I have been informed in detail about this and I accept all kinds of medical and surgical
treatment, taking into account all the information given above.


I also agree / disagree with shaving all or part of my hair. I also consent to the use of the photographs I
took before, during and after the treatment for scientific purposes, provided that my identity is kept confidential.


During the recovery period after the operation, attention should be paid to the following issues.


The healing process of the wounds is between 4-10 days.


Forehead swelling may occur from the 3rd to the 5th day.


The feeling of numbness in the area from which the hair is taken (donor) may last from 3 weeks to
several months.
